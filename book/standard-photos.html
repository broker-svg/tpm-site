Good to Know

Common questions.

How should I prepare the property?
Declutter, turn all lights on, open blinds, and make beds. I'll handle the rest on-site — small staging touches as needed.
When will I receive the photos?
Within 24 hours of the shoot, delivered via Dropbox — ready to upload to MLS.
Do I need to be on-site?
Not required, but appreciated. If the seller lets us in and the property is prepped, you don't need to attend.
What if the weather is bad?
We'll reschedule the exterior portion if needed and can return for exteriors only. No charge for weather reschedules.
